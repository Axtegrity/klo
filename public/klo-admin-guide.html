Managing Your Vault & Homepage

Step-by-step instructions for uploading content to the Vault and editing your homepage — everything you need in one place.

Part One

Uploading Content to the Vault

The Vault is your library — articles, videos, guides, frameworks, and more. Members access it based on their subscription tier. Here's how to add and manage everything.

Getting There
  1. 1
    Go to app.keithlodom.io/admin and log in with your admin credentials.
  2. 2
    Click the Content tab in the top navigation bar.
  3. 3
    You'll see two sections — Vault and Feed. Make sure you're in the Vault section.
Adding a New Item
  1. 1
    Click the + New Item button in the top right of the Vault section. A form will open.
  2. 2
    Fill in the fields below, then click Save.

Managing Existing Items
🔍 Search bar — find items by title
👁 Published / Hidden filter toggle
⚡ Visibility toggle on each item
✏️ Pencil icon — edit any item
🗑 Trash icon — delete permanently
🔄 Refresh button (circular arrow)
📦
Archived items: Look for the archived folder at the bottom of the list. Items here are removed from the live Vault but saved. You can republish any archived item at any time — useful for seasonal or time-sensitive content.
💡
Hiding vs. deleting: Use the visibility toggle to hide an item temporarily — it won't appear to members but stays in your dashboard. Only use the trash icon if you're sure you no longer need it.

Part Two

Editing Your Homepage

The homepage editor lets you update images and content across your site in a live preview — what you see is what your visitors will see. No coding required.

Getting There
  1. 1
    From the admin dashboard, make sure you're on the Overview tab (the first tab you land on).
  2. 2
    Click the blue Edit Home Page button near the top — it has a wand icon and reads "Edit Home Page — click any image to change it."
  3. 3
    The live preview editor will open in full screen.
The Editor Bar

A sticky bar sits at the top of the editor with all your controls:

Editing Home Page Unsaved
Save Status
Shows "Saved" or "Unsaved" with a pulsing gold dot when you have changes waiting to be published.
Viewport Toggle
Switch between Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop to see how your page looks on each screen size.
Discard
Throws away any changes you haven't published. Grayed out when nothing has changed yet.
Publish
Saves your changes and pushes them live to the site. Shows "Saving…" then "Published ✓".
Exit
Returns you to the admin dashboard. Will warn you if you have unsaved changes.
Advanced
Opens deeper customization options in Creative Studio. Visible on desktop only.
Changing an Image
  1. 1
    In the preview, click on any image that has an edit overlay (a pencil or camera icon that appears when you hover over it).
  2. 2
    A file picker will open. Select your new image from your computer and confirm.
  3. 3
    The preview updates immediately. The Unsaved indicator appears in the top bar with a pulsing gold dot.
  4. 4
    Click Publish in the top right when you're ready to push the change live.
⚠️
Always publish before exiting. If you click Exit with unpublished changes, you'll see a prompt: "You have unsaved changes. Exit without publishing?" — clicking Yes will discard your work.
💡
Quick Tips
  • Always click Publish before leaving the editor. Clicking Exit without publishing will prompt a warning — confirm Yes and your changes are gone.
  • Use the visibility toggle, not the trash icon, when you want to hide a Vault item temporarily. You can make it live again at any time without re-creating it.
  • When setting Access Tier, remember: Free means every visitor can see it. Anything above Free requires a matching paid subscription — set it intentionally.
